What is Veece?

Veece (pronounced “people”) is a non-bank digital app that gives everyone access to the financial system through a smartphone. With an internationally accepted prepaid Visa, conducting transactions with Veece is as simple as sending a message.

How It Works?

Veece is for everyone, no bank account needed, no minimum salary, no minimum balance, no post-dated cheques, no paperwork, and no branch visit.
